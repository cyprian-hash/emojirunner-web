PRIVACY POLICY

Your data stays on your device.

Last updated: June 5, 2026

The short version

Emoji Runner does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. Everything you do in the game stays on your device.

Information we collect

The app stores the following on your device using Apple's standard local storage (UserDefaults):

  • Your personal best high score
  • Total diamonds collected across all runs
  • Sound effects preference (on / off)
  • Haptics preference (on / off)

This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not transmitted to us or to any third party
  • Is deleted automatically when you uninstall the app

What we do not collect

  • Your name, email address, or any other identifier
  • Your location
  • Your IP address
  • Device identifiers (IDFA, advertising ID)
  • Crash logs or analytics
  • Any usage data

Game Center

If you sign in to Game Center, Apple handles your identity and any leaderboard data per Apple's Privacy Policy. Emoji Runner only receives an anonymous player identifier from Apple and never your real name or email.

Children

Emoji Runner is rated 4+ and is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any data from any user, including children.

Changes to this policy

If we ever change this policy, we'll update this page and revise the "Last updated" date above.

Contact

Questions? Email us at support@emojirunner.com.
